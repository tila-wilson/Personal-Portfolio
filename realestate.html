Tila Wilson

It's a family thing.

Having grown up in a family of contactors and developing my skills in organization and project management it was a natural transition into investing in the real estate market. Remodeling homes is a way to fulfill my creative itch and provide jobs and housing to my family. We mainly choose homes in the West Central Neighborhood to improve what we believe to be a diamond in the rough. The homes in West Central are very diverse, and the proximity to downtown and college campuses is in my opinion the right place to get in on the ground floor in terms of investments. Each person in my family has a specialized skill that assists in remodels and general maintenance. More than the potential gain, the memories I am creating with my family and the improvements we are making to this neighborhood make this a very enjoyable hobby.
